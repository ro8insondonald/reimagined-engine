Phillip Schofield speaks out after 'toxic' This Morning claims

He's one of daytime TV's most-loved presenters but recent reports suggest it's not all smiles and fits of giggles for This Morning's Phillip Schofield. The host of the ITV show has come under criticism from fellow ITV star Amanda Holden , who reportedly claimed Phil "cost" her a presenting role on This Morning - branding her "hard to manage." She also raised her concerns with ITV chief Kevin Lygo, according to the Mirror . However, Schofe took to Twitter to defend himself, hoping that his fans "know him better". The lovable host tweeted: "The end of another really sad weekend. "When you try for 35 years to be the easiest, most fun person to work with and you read such hurtful and wildly untrue stories from nameless ‘sources’. "Obviously I’ll take it on the chin.. I just hope you know me better."
